# Decision

Wait to wash the car.

# Reasons

The best car wash decision depends on whether the cleaning will stay useful for at least a short window. If rain, snow, road spray, construction dust, or pollen is likely soon, washing immediately has low value because the car will look dirty again quickly.

A wash is worth doing today only if visibility or safety is affected, for example dirty windows, salt on lights, heavy grime on sensors, or residue that could damage paint. Cosmetic dirt alone does not justify washing when conditions will undo the result.

# Action

Do a quick spot clean today: windshield, mirrors, lights, cameras, and license plate. Schedule a full exterior wash for the next dry day with at least 24 hours of clean driving expected. If there is road salt or corrosive residue on the car, choose a basic rinse now and save the detail wash for later.
